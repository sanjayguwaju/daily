Introduction to Cow

The cow is a domestic animal which is considered useful to humans. It is used to provide us with various things that are of great importance such as milk, meat, and skin. The cow is a large four-legged animal that has a large body with a hump on its back. Its skin is covered with hair. Cows are found in various colors such as white, brown, and black. Some cows are also spotted or striped.

The scientific name of the cow is Bos Taurus and are the members of Bovinae. The family also includes Gazelles, Buffalo, Bison, Antelopes and Goats. In many countries like India, cows are considered sacred, but in many countries, some breeds of cow are used as a dish for consumption.
